BD2K Open Educational Resources

Open Educational Resources covering various topics in data science are available in the form of online materials that are available here, that are freely available to educators and students. This project was funded by an award to the OHSU Library and the Department of Medical Informatics & Clinical Epidemiology (DMICE), as part of the NIH Big Data to Knowledge (BD2K) initiative, with the goal of improving health research by training students and researchers to better utilize and generate knowledge from large biomedical datasets.

Funding: The BD2K OERs are funded by NIH grant # 1R25GM114820.

Ontologies 101 - online training

This online tutorial covers basics in biomedical ontology development using the ontology software, Protege. Feedback is welcome via our GitHub tracker.

Funding: The project was funded in part by the BD2K OER NIH grant # 1R25GM114820.
